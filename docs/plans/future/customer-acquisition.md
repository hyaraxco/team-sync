# Customer Acquisition — Team Sync SaaS

> Strategy untuk mendapatkan first 100 customers di Indonesia.

---

## Channels

### 1. Content Marketing (Organic)

- **HR Blog**: Tips HR, payroll Indonesia, labor law guides (UU Ketenagakerjaan, BPJS, PPh21)
- **SEO Target**: "software HR Indonesia", "aplikasi payroll", "HRIS Indonesia"
- **Platform**: Blog + LinkedIn articles + Medium
- **Frequency**: 2 artikel/minggu

### 2. Community (Social)

- **Facebook Groups**: HR Indonesia, HR Manager Community, Payroll Indonesia
- **LinkedIn**: Connect dengan HR managers, post case studies
- **Strategy**: Share free tools (template payroll Excel, checklist onboarding) → build trust → convert

### 3. Referral Program

- Existing customer referral: diskon 1 bulan untuk referrer + referee
- Milestone reward: 5 referral → 1 bulan gratis, 10 referral → 3 bulan gratis
- Track via unique referral link per customer

### 4. Free Tier as Funnel

- Free tier (25 karyawan) = product-led growth
- In-app upgrade prompts saat user capai limit atau butuh fitur Pro
- Email nurture sequence: Day 1, 3, 7, 14 (trial end)

### 5. Partnerships

- **Accounting firms**: Bundle Team Sync dengan jasa akuntansi mereka
- **Payroll consultants**: Commission-based referral (10% first year)
- **Coworking spaces**: Demo day, free trial untuk members
- **HR communities**: Sponsor events, workshops

| Attribute | Ideal Customer |

| Location | Jakarta, Bandung, Surabaya, Bali |
